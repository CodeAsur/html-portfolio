The Best Movies According to Karthikeya

My top 3 Movies of all time

Jersey

A heartful story about a middle-aged retired cricket player restarting his cricket career, all for his son. Written and directed by Goutham Tinnanuri with Nani,Shradha Srinath,Sathyaraj in lead roles.

Athadu

Nanda Gopal, a professional hitman, is wrongly framed in an assassination. During his escape on a train,the police wrongly shoot an innocent man,Pardhu, instead of the protagonist. This leads Nandu to go to Pardhu's home in a small village to report his demise only to be mistakened as the real Pardhu. One of Trivikram's and Mahesh Babu's best works.

Bahubali 2

Rajamouli's magnum opus and sequel to the game-changer of indian cinema. The story is set in ancient india in a kingdom called Mahishmati. Mahendra Bahubali, our protagonist must find his origin and his father's past to take his rightful place of emperor from his evil uncle. The film stars Prabhas,Anushka Shetty,Rana Daggubatti,Tamannah Bhatia and many others in lead roles.
